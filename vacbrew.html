Condensed Steam Vacuum Technology

Condensed steam creates high vacuum in about 2 seconds. The high vacuum pulls water through the coffee grounds. Fresh, rich, healthy, and tasty cold coffee can be extracted within 5–60 minutes depending on the size of the coffee maker.

Because the coffee is extracted in vacuum, there is no oxidation during the process.
